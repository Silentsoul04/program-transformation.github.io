Disinformation Decompiler {#disinformation-decompiler .twikiTopicTitle}
=========================

::: {.twikiWebTitle}
Program-Transformation.Org: The Program Transformation Wiki
:::

Disinformation is a decompiler for Z-machine data files (Infocom or
Inform), to decompile Infocom games to add rooms and objects to the
game. It works best on Inform 6 (library 6/7), due to the debugging
information available in such files. It can still be downloaded from the
[Inform Z-code tools](http://www.inform-fiction.org/zmachine/zcode.html)
page. The original page was at
<http://www.homeusers.prestel.co.uk/lwtcdi/uninform/instruct.htm>, and
can still be accessed from
[archive.org](http://web.archive.org/web/*/http://www.homeusers.prestel.co.uk/lwtcdi/uninform/instruct.htm).
