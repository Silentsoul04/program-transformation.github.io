Cppx {#cppx .twikiTopicTitle}
====

::: {.twikiWebTitle}
Program-Transformation.Org: The Program Transformation Wiki
:::

CPPX is a free, open source fact extractor for C++. It is build on top
of [GNU](GNU){.twikiLink} g++, and produces a graph according to the
Datrix fact model, in either [GXL](GXL){.twikiLink}, TA, or VCG format.

<http://swag.uwaterloo.ca/~cppx/>
