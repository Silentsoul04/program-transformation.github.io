[]{#Reported_Platforms} Reported Platforms
------------------------------------------

-   GNU/Linux at i386
    -   SuSE 8.2 and 9.0
    -   Redhat 8.0
    -   Redhat 9.0
    -   Debian 3.0

<!-- -->

-   GNU/Linux at PowerPC
    -   Debian testing with GCC 3.4.1, PowerMac6,3

<!-- -->

-   Mac OS X
    -   10.3.4 at PowerPC, using GCC 3.3

<!-- -->

-   FreeBSD at i386

<!-- -->

-   Cygwin at Microsoft Windows XP
    -   version 1.5.7 has been tested.
