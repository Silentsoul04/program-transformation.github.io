GpceWorkshops {#gpceworkshops .twikiTopicTitle}
=============

::: {.twikiWebTitle}
Generative Programming and Component Engineering
:::

Workshops are full day and take place on October 4, 2007. Registration
instructions for workshops will be announced when conference
registration opens.

GPCE\'07 presents the following workshops:

\* [APGES\'07: Workshop on Automatic Program Generation for Embedded
Systems](APGES07){.twikiLink} \* [AOPLE\'07: Second Workshop on
Aspect-Oriented Product Line Engineering](AOPLE07){.twikiLink}

Workshops/Tutorials committee:

\* [Ulrik P. Schultz, Workshop/Tutorials
Chair](http://www.mmmi.sdu.dk/~ups) (University of Southern Denmark,
Denmark) \* [Remi Douence](http://www.emn.fr/x-info/douence/) (Ecole des
Mines de Nantes-Inria, Lina, France) \* [Christa
Schwanninger](http://www.kircher-schwanninger.de/christa/) (Siemens,
Germany) \* [Peter Sestoft](http://www.dina.kvl.dk/~sestoft/) (IT
University of Copenhagen, Denmark)\
